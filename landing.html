Projects Overview

On this page, you'll find blurbs on the various projects I've worked on. Click on one to read more.

Dust-Obscured Star Formation with SIMBA

We used the SIMBA 25 Mpc/h fiducial volume with its dust model and 3D radiative transfer run with POWDERDAY to model how much star formation is obscured by dust across cosmic time.

  • Learn more

SED Modeling with Cosmological Galaxy Simulations

We used a wide variety of simulation model volumes and radiative transfer to build a simulation-based inference (SBI) pipeline to recover galaxy properties from observations. Our model outperformed traditional SED fitting strategies.

  • Learn more

High-z Attenuation Curves with SMUGGLE

We ran many zoom-in galaxies with SMUGGLE physics (explicit ISM, self-consistently evolving dust grain size distribution) and ran 3D radiative transfer on them to study what attenuation curves look like in the early Universe. Paper hopefully out soon!
